When reserving a unit from a builder, it's common for the builder to provide a set of terms and conditions to outline the rights and responsibilities of both parties involved in the transaction. These terms and conditions can vary depending on the specific builder and the real estate market in your area, but here are some common clauses and provisions you might find in such an agreement: Reservation Deposit: The amount of the reservation deposit required to secure the unit, as well as the conditions under which it is refundable or forfeited. Price and Payment Terms: The total purchase price of the unit, the payment schedule, and any applicable taxes, fees, or charges. Completion Date: The estimated or guaranteed date when the unit will be ready for occupancy or ownership transfer. Specifications and Features: Details about the specifications, features, and finishes of the unit, including any allowances for customization or upgrades Back
